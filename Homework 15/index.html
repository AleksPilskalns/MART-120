    • Background

    The game a Day in the Life of a Simp (part 7) came about when amatuer programmer Aleks needed to create an assignment for week 4 of the creative coding class. The assignment required use of Code.Org's' app lab. Aleks ended up creating a rather unconventional game. A choose your own story adventure but as a spoof of the dating simulator genre. The romance interests in the game include characters that are ironically characters that nobody would actually choose to date, including but not limited to, a tree, and Shadow the Hedgehog from the game franchise Sonic The Hedgehog. At least, one hopes nobody would choose to date them.

    It is reported that amateur coder Aleks chose this particular idea for their assignment purely because they thought it would be funny, and because their friend told them to.


    Gameplay

    The gameplay includes clicking buttons to choose which option you want to pick on your choose your own adventure. Very thrilling stuff.


    Fun Facts

  • Despite being named with a (part 7) there aren't actually any prequals
